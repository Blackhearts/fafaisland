About Fafa Island Resort

Discover our story and commitment to exceptional hospitality

Our History

Founded in 1990, Fafa Island Resort began as a tranquil hideaway on a small private island just off the coast of Tongatapu. With only a handful of traditional fale-inspired bungalows, the resort was created to showcase Tonga’s natural beauty and warm hospitality.Over the years, Fafa Island Resort has grown into a celebrated destination for travelers seeking peace, authenticity, and connection with the ocean.

Guided by the values of respect for nature and Tongan culture, the resort continues to welcome guests from around the world to experience the perfect balance of island simplicity, comfort, and unforgettable sunsets.

Our Philosophy

At Fafa Island Resort, we believe true luxury is rooted in simplicity and connection — to nature, to culture, and to each other. From our traditionally inspired bungalows to the heartfelt hospitality of our staff, every detail of your stay is designed to create lasting memories.

We are dedicated to sustainable tourism and honoring the spirit of Tonga. Our practices include the use of renewable energy, water conservation, and active support for local communities and conservation projects. By caring for the land and sea, we ensure that Fafa Island remains a sanctuary of beauty for generations to come.
